Common Questions

Registration FAQs

Do I need to be a member to register?

No! Our events are open to everyone. You don't need to be a member to participate in workshops, photowalks, and exhibitions.

What should I bring?

It depends on the event. Most events welcome any camera (DSLR, mirrorless, film, or smartphone). Check the event description for specific requirements.

Is there a registration fee?

Most of our events are free for everyone. Special workshops may have a nominal fee, which will be mentioned in the event details.

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely! Each registration allows up to 5 attendees. Just indicate the number when registering.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the event. Simply email us at kuetphotosociety@gmail.com.

How will I receive event details?

We'll send you confirmation and detailed instructions via email after you register.

Questions about an event?

Reach out to us directly for more information
